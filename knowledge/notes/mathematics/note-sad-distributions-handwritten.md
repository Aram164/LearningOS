# Statistik Kapitel 05–08 — handwritten working notes

**What this is:** the second volume of Aram's handwritten series (`Teil 02`,
21 pp, German, scanned Oct 2025). Opens with "Diskrete Verteilungen — Kapitel
05": the Binomial as Bernoulli experiment (Galton-board intuition), definition
X ~ Binomial(n,p) with P(X=x), E(X)=np, Var(X)=np(1−p), and — exam-gold — a
self-made "Schlüsselwörter für Aufgabe" checklist for recognizing Binomial vs
hypergeometric setups (mit/ohne Zurücklegen, p konstant vs ändernd). Later
pages: Normal-distribution problems worked in full — standardization,
inverse-Φ lookups (solving for σ from P(X<22)=0.05), and "Blatt 04 Aufgabe 01 —
2025" (P(160≤X≤170) via Φ((b−μ)/σ) − Φ((a−μ)/σ), symmetry rule 1−Φ(1)).
